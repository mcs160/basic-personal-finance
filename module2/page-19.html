Insurance 101.3

Summary

In this lesson we reviewed what the purpose of an insurance policy is and why it is important that we have it as a means to protect our net worth. We also explored a little more detail about what net worth is and how it plays into how you choose what insurance limits you should carry. You also got to see some images that provide you an example of how devastating some losses can be, and how insurance can help people recover. Below are some questions to think as you continue through this learning module.

  • What is my net worth?

  • What coverage options are available?
  • What are the different policy types available?
