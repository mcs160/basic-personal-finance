Insurance 102.3

Summary

In this lesson we reviewed many of the rating factors that can influence the rates you pay for insurance. As discussed, you have control over many of these factors and more companies are offering telematics technology that provide you even more control. Things like devices that you plug into your car to report your driving habits, like rapid acceleration or sudden stops. This way you only pay for the risk you hold and not the average in your area. Some things to think about as you continue to the next lesson:

  • How can I improve my rating factors on my insurance?
  • How do these factors affect my bottom dollar?
  • What type of policies do I need?
