There are Five 'C's to Creditworthiness that are looked at by lenders.

Capacity

This is determined by your income. Can you afford to make the required payments?


Capital

How much of your own money do you have invested in the transaction?


Collateral

What asset, if any, are you pledging as guarantee for repayment, a house, a car, etc.?


Condition

What is the purpose of the loan, house to live in, a car to drive to work in, money to start a business, or simply personal use?


Character

What impression have you given to the lender? This opinion will be based on a number of things including but not limited to your educational background, your employment history, and your credit score.
