Captions

Closed-end products, as mentioned before, are used for specific purposes to finance large items. Due to their expense, cars, boats, motorcycles, airplanes, and real estate are often out of reach for buyers without credit therefore lenders allow the debt to be guaranteed by collateral. Legal ownership, or title to the property given as collateral is surrendered at the onset of the loan. Upon successful repayment, title is returned. If the debt is not repaid, the collateral is repossessed by the lender or their appointee. Risk for the lender is low due to the collateral; therefore, interests rates are commonly quite low.
