Credit 101.4

Audio


Captions

The information held by the credit bureaus is evaluated by their proprietary formulas into a score, or FICO score. This term is derived from the name of a company, Fair Isaac Corporation, that has developed and distributed credit score calculation software.

Your score along this scale will determine your creditworthiness as judged by the credit bureaus. This scale is used by lenders to assist in determining creditworthiness and in most cases determines the interest rate and amount offered.
