Credit 102.1

Audio


Captions

There are two categories under which credit products are classified: Closed-end and Revolving.

Closed-end products are used for a specific purpose. For example, a house, a car, an airplane, etc. These loans are repaid on installments and typically require title to the item financed to be given as collateral for the loan.

Revolving credit products can be repeatedly used, normally within a preset limit and are repaid on a monthly basis. The most common type of revolving credit is the credit card. Let us explore each of the a little deeper.
