Captions

Your credit score is a number assigned by these credit bureaus based on the following qualities: Payment History, Amount Owed, Length of Credit History, Types of Credit In Use, and New Credit or Inquiries.


Lenders report this information to the three credit bureaus: Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion. Those inquiring about your credit may elect to use any or all information gained from these bureaus to assist in evaluating your character.
