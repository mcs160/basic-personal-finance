Direct Deposit

You can use deposit and withdrawal slips for checking accounts as well, but direct deposit is also a convenient option. This allows employers to electronically deposit your paycheck into your checking account so your funds are immediately available.

A checking account is an everyday money management tool that you use for regular expenses. When you deposit money in your checking account, you can use your funds to make a purchase or pay bills with a check, debit card, or electronically. You can also get cash from the ATM machine using your ATM or debit card. You will need to keep track of how much money you deposit into your account, and how much to keep in your account on a regular basis.
